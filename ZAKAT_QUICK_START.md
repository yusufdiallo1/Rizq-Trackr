## How It Works (4 Simple Steps)

### Step 1: Calculate Your Zakatable Wealth ‚úÖ

The calculator automatically shows:

1. **Your Current Savings**: $X,XXX
   - Calculated from: Income - Expenses - Zakat Paid
   - Updates automatically

2. **Your Zakatable Income**: $X,XXX

   - Only mark income held for one full year

3. **Your Debts**: Enter amount

   - Reduces your zakatable wealth

## Common Questions ‚ùì

**Q: When should I mark income as zakatable?**
A: After it has been in your possession for one full lunar year (Hawl).

**Q: What if my calculation seems wrong?**
A: Check that all income/expenses are recorded and zakatable status is correct.

**Q: Can I edit a payment?**
A: Currently no - record payments carefully. You can add a new payment with corrected amount.

**Q: What's included in savings?**
A: Total Income - Total Expenses - Total Zakat Paid = Current Savings

**Q: How often should I pay Zakat?**
A: Once per lunar year when your wealth exceeds Nisab for a full year.
